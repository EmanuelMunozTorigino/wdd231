Residential Solar Panel Systems

Learn about the benefits and capacities of different solar panel systems for your home.

Small System: 2 kW - 3 kW

Monthly Production: 240 - 360 kWh

Usage: Ideal for small apartments or homes with 1-2 people.

Equipment: LED lighting, small refrigerator, fans, laptop, TV, and light appliances.

Medium System: 4 kW - 5 kW

Monthly Production: 480 - 600 kWh

Usage: Suitable for medium-sized homes with 3-4 people.

Equipment: Large refrigerator, air conditioner (1-2 units), washer, TV, lighting, and common appliances.

Large System: 6 kW - 8 kW

Monthly Production: 720 - 960 kWh

Usage: Ideal for larger homes with high energy consumption.

Equipment: Multiple air conditioners, dishwasher, water pumps, large appliances, and entertainment systems.

Extra Large System: 10 kW - 12 kW

Monthly Production: 1,200 - 1,500 kWh

Usage: Suitable for very large homes or homes with high-demand equipment (e.g., heated pools or electric heating).

Equipment: All of the above, plus industrial or additional electrical equipment.

Factors Affecting Production

  • Geographical Location: Higher solar radiation in sunnier areas (e.g., northern Argentina) increases production.
  • Panel Orientation and Tilt: Panels facing north (in the southern hemisphere) with optimal tilt maximize efficiency.
  • Weather Conditions: Clouds, dust, or shading from trees and buildings can reduce efficiency.
  • Maintenance: Regular cleaning of panels improves production.

Equipment Consumption Comparison

  • Large Refrigerator: ~30-50 kWh/month
  • Air Conditioner (2.5 kW): ~100-150 kWh/month (used 5 hours/day)
  • 42" LED TV: ~15-20 kWh/month
  • Water Pump: ~60-100 kWh/month (depending on use)
  • Complete LED Lighting: ~20-30 kWh/month (medium-sized home)

Recommendations for people who lives in Argentina

A typical household in Argentina consumes between 300 and 600 kWh per month, so a 4-6 kW solar system could cover most of the monthly consumption, depending on efficiency and usage patterns. In addition, to avoid overconsumption, it is advisable to combine the solar system with energy-efficient equipment (such as LED lighting and efficient appliances).
